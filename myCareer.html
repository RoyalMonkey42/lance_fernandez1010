T E A C H I N G   M A T H E M A T I C S


As for my career after I gradute college, I want to teach high school math.
However, I did not always want to become a math teacher. Growing up, my eyes
were set on becoming a mechanical engineer. When I declared my major, I chose
the Engineering Physics program here at Vanguard. During my classes though, it
opened my eyes that I would not really be happy with the career path I chose.
My appreciation for math started in high school and the thought of becoming
a teacher actually only came to mind during my senior year of high school because
I was inspired by my AP Calculus teacher, Mr. Lundstrom.

In school, I would always hear how people hate math and do poorly on it.
I went through the very same thing until I had a good math teacher. This is
what inspired me to switch my major to Mathematics and pursue being a math
teacher. Countless of students struggle with math because they have a bad
instructor and learn to hate the subject. I want to change that though so
people can end up appreciating math like I do.
