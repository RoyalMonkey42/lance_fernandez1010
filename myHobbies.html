C H E S S


My grandfather introduced me to chess when I was around 10 years old
and I found it really interesting! I used to play with him a lot until
I started playing my brothers. Chess became a little competition we would
have to see who was the best. Earlier on in my life, I would say my older
brother was the best at chess. However, around 8th grade, I started playing
more seriously. I won chess competitions at a few of my schools and ended up
beating my older brother.

More recently, I have been wanting to take chess to the next level. I am
playing a whole lot more, watching youtube videos on how the grandmasters
play, and looking at chess strategies so I can improve even more as a player.
I even watched the Netflix show, The Queen's Gambit ,
and was even inspired more to get better at the game.
